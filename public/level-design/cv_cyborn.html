Profile

I'm a level designer who completed CGMA's "Level Design for Games" and have 7 years of professional software-development experience. I build levels in Unreal Engine and Unity, focusing on composition, believable scale and player guidance, and grounding every space in real reference so it reads clearly and feels like a real place. I script my own systems in Blueprint and code, so I don't have to wait for a programmer to bring a design to life.
